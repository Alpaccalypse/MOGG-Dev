creature_dairy_generic

		!! These "creatures" contain only the material definitions for different generic dairy products based on broad mammal categories. These are purely for use in referencing dairy in other creatures, to keep the various dairy products in more concise categories rather than defining each on on a per-creature level. The MILKABLE tags are commented out with a # in place of the first bracket, purely to keep the conventional structure represented. !!
			!! You can see examples of how these can be used in c_variation_dairy and the creature raws that are referencing those variations !!
